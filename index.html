About Us

Apple of God Foundation is a charitable arm of the Apple of God Group, dedicated to transforming lives through love, education, and opportunity. Founded on the core belief that "Let Love Lead," our mission is to support underserved communities—especially children—by providing access to quality education and essential learning materials.

Our Programs

  • Children from poor or single-parent homes.
  • Pupils in rural or underserved communities.
  • Orphans and vulnerable children.
